# BUSINESS REQUIREMENT DOCUMENT

## Project Name

Hotel Management and Online Reservation System with AI Assistant and Electronic Invoice

---

## Objective

Develop a modern web-based hotel management platform that supports:

* Online room reservation
* Hotel operations management
* Electronic invoice generation
* AI-powered customer assistance
* Role-based access control
* Subscription-based premium features
* Bilingual Vietnamese-English interface

---

## Actors

### Guest

Unregistered visitor.

Can:

* Browse rooms
* Search rooms
* View hotel information

Cannot:

* Make reservations
* Access dashboard

---

### Customer

Can:

* Register
* Login
* Reserve rooms
* Cancel reservations
* View reservation history
* Download invoices
* Use AI assistant (Premium)

---

### Receptionist

Can:

* View reservations
* Check-in customers
* Check-out customers
* Generate invoices

---

### Hotel Admin

Can:

* Manage rooms
* Manage room types
* Manage services
* Manage reservations
* Manage invoices
* View statistics

---

### Super Admin

Can:

* Manage all hotels
* Manage subscriptions
* Manage users
* Manage permissions
* Manage AI settings
